Kuku Melekote is committed to protecting your privacy and your child's personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard data when you use our educational app designed for children aged 3-. We collect minimal information necessary to provide educational services: - Child's age group for age-appropriate content delivery - Learning progress data to track educational milestones - App usage analytics to improve functionality - Parental account information for subscription management - Device information for technical support - Screen time data for parental controls We do NOT collect personal identifying information from children under 13. All data collection complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and international child privacy regulations. Information is used exclusively to provide personalized educational experiences, track learning progress, recommend appropriate content based on age group, improve app functionality, and manage subscription services.
